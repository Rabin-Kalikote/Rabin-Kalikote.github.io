ABOUT ME

Hello everybody! I'm Rabin. I was born on May 2, 2001 AD. I'm from Syabun, Sankhuwasabha that is a beautiful village of Nepal. During my childhood I was in the same place playing and enjoying with the nature of my beautiful Village. With my great luck, I was admitted in Surya Sec. Boarding School. I didn't know why I was there but when I studied there for some years I realized that what I can do. I have been continiously learning in the same school. Now I'm 10th grader at SSBS. During my some year of learing, I have learned many things in the diverse subjects. Among them arts, science and computer are my favourites.
Though I left my beautiful village, I a'm not so sad because I got many things being in Khandbari. I was able to write a story book expressed all by poems (Jindagiko Baato) that is my own creation. I have learned a lot about scientifical experiences and facts. In the other side computer programming is equally exciting. So, I'm much excited with my student life.
