I'm more than a Resume!

I love to build solutions that solve real problems. In 2018, I discovered that not all students have access to library with high quality academic materials. So, I decided partner with my academically briliant friends and juniors to create the largerst online library of notes, questions and answers, creative and research articles, and education guides. I created the app, and in few months, it became the popolarly used social learning app with 10,000 registered students.

Ask Mattrab is AI powered social learning platform, offering free high-quality academic notes, question-answers, entrance exam preparation materials, and USA education guides. Imagine an app for students with their school, friends, and learning materials. That's Ask Mattrab.

I like to exercise my coding muscle and brain-cells. In spring 2024, I built AgriNews for some exercises. Interestingly, it brought me an interview to Data and Product Engineering Internship.

AgriNews scrapes news from 4 Nepali websites, filters agriculture related news, and hosts the latest news in one website.

I enjoy teaching what I learn. Isn't it fun to simplify learning by removing complexity and adding humor? In Fall 2024, I instructed "Build-Your-Brand 101" where I taught web development basics as well as guided the creation of personal websites.

Build-Your-Brand 101 is a week long workshop to build and host the personal website online.

In my free time, I write poems. I imagine stories. I play with words. This is probably why my girlfriend is imaginary.

'Jindagiko Baato' is a multi-act play with the story expressed in poems. I completed wrting it when I was 14 years old.

In my YouTube channel, Friend of Computer, I have taught full stack web development to over 70,000 people in Nepali language. Talking to a camera is fun!
